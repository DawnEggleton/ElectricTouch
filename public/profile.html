Cheatsheet

  • Various scars, including: cigarette burns along his forearms, scattered slices across his back, a gash along his lower left abdomen (side of ribcage to belly button), a large lichtenberg figure radiating from right collarbone, a smaller matching starburst on his left ankle, and a bullet wound centered directly over his heart. He still gets stiff and sore in his shoulder and ankle to this day.

  • No matter the weather, he will be wearing long-sleeved shirts to cover the scars. He remains ashamed of them and uncomfortable discussing his history, especially in regards to them.

  • Started with a degree in electrical engineering in his youth, leading to code-breaking assistance during the Second World War. However, after this, he floundered a little and found little joy in his original field of study, so he has gone on to get a couple different undergraduate degrees, including computer sciences, music, psychology, and literature.

  • More recently, he attended medical school and completed his residency as a trauma surgeon out of a desire to be prepared; he's seen war and he wants to be ready this time.

  • Much like his studies, he's also been picking up a variety of languages over the years. His mother tongue is Serbian, but he is fluent in Croatian, English, French, and Italian; conversational in Russian, German, and Spanish; and he is contemplating picking up something else now. He also has some classical Latin training, though he wouldn't dare proclaim himself an expert.

  • After the storm when he was younger, he's been wary of thunder and lightning since. He has his coping mechanisms, but you're never going to find him outside in a storm unless he absolutely has to be - and the more he can drown out the sound and sight of it, the better.

  • He never quite gained proper control of all of his abilities and he has been known to accidentally light small fires in times of high emotion - of any kind, even positive, though it is far more likely to happen when he is anxious or stressed.

  • He's picked up a fair few self-soothing hobbies over the years, but the most obvious are the piano and the fact he stress-bakes.... a lot. The hospital staff have all put on an extra couple pounds since he returned.

  • He has a deep affinity for high places and feeling the wind in his face. The fact he can fly and cannot die both help encourage him taking stupid risks to chase down this high, and it isn't uncommon to see him at the very edge of a cliff or atop some of the taller buildings in town.

  • He's a hell of an insomniac and a workaholic - given his job, that's. a damn-near deadly combination. He has gone days without sleeping in more than fifteen minute naps, and he's done it frequently. If he could die, it might well have killed him by now.

  • He also has an eidetic memory which, while it has helped with all of the above academic endeavours... it also makes it extremely difficult to forget what he's been through and what he's done. Parts of his life have been haunting enough as it is; he doesn't need the reminders.
Interested

  • Found family plots (the kelpies who found him coming back to town or otherwise)

  • Helping get him out of his shell and more comfortable in his skin, because he still isn't that

  • Fellow medical staff to hang out with, especially any who are longer-lived as he's a little gun-shy about getting too attached to humans and those with human lifespans
Not Interested

  • Romance is already sorted and he isn't the sort to have prior flings

  • Enemies of any kind- he's had enough heartache and he'd just bend over backwards for them anyway so it'd be a little dry

  • You're not super likely to find him embroiled in any sort of nightlife or large celebrations that draw crowds, either - try a library or hiking trail instead
Overview Nik is kind of a walking tragedy, to be quite honest. He's had a really hard life, and being semi-immortal hasn't really helped much, because it's given him even more time to experience these hardships. He's a sensitive soul, feeling each of these things very deeply and to extremes, despite his attempts to shove all those feelings down. He is kind, though, and unerringly so. He will attempt to aide just about anyone who needs it, even at great personal risk or cost to his own well-being. You'll find he can be quite distant at first if he doesn't know you. He is quite cautious, despite his desire to help and heal, and so will take a while to warm up, but he's trying to work on it.

When it comes to interacting with people, he's particularly soft-spoken, but an otherwise excellent conversationalist (assuming you can get him to speak). He's easily distractable, often getting caught up in a flurry of his own thoughts and ideas, but trust that he hasn't missed a word you're saying either (at least, usually). He just might take a second or two to catch up and backtrack to what you were talking about first. Most of the people he knows, he knows through his work as a doctor in the emergency room, where he's been practising for just over ten years now.
